Batch gift entry

Keyboard-first

  • Tab moves between cells
  • Enter creates a new row
  • Ctrl+D duplicates the previous row
  • Errors turn rose · warnings amber · valid green

What happens on Post

Valid rows post immediately. Invalid rows split into a new exception batch (Arlington feedback) — you can't edit a posted batch.

4
Resolve duplicate constituents
Screen: atlas-duplicates.html → atlas-merge.html
Open live →
Duplicate review · 9 pending

Match confidence

  • ≥ 95% high confidence — usually safe to auto-merge
  • 85–94% needs human review (most common)
  • 75–84% likely false positive — check carefully
  • Every merge is reversible for 30 days

The merge flow

Side-by-side compare → field-level keep/replace → audit note required → save. The deleted record is preserved for reverse-merge.

Open live →
Receipt preview · R-2026-0148

The 6 compliance checks

  • Donor name & address present
  • Date of contribution shown
  • Amount stated (or "shares only" for stock)
  • "No goods or services" disclosure
  • Legal-entity sender identity
  • Authorized signature embedded

When all 6 pass, Atlas allows the send.
