What kind of campaign are you launching?

This drives the recommended hierarchy template and which receipt rules apply.

Capital campaign
Multi-year capital project with named-gift recognition. Big asks, longer timelines, naming opportunities.
e.g. Cathedral restoration · new school wing · scholarship endowment
Template: Major-donor cultivation · Online giving · Gala
Annual appeal
Yearly recurring appeal — seasonal mailings, online forms, pledge drives.
e.g. Annual Appeal 2026 · Christmas appeal · Easter offering
Template: Spring mailing · Year-end · Online giving
Special / event
Single-event campaign — gala, retreat, or one-off project with a fixed end date.
e.g. Bishop's Annual Gala · Refugee Aid emergency · Building dedication
Template: Sponsorships · Tickets · Online donation
Memorial / planned giving
Bequest cultivation with deferred-pledge tracking. Multi-decade timelines.
e.g. Heritage Society · estate-gift outreach · planned-giving campaign
Template: Heritage Society · Pledge intentions · Stewardship
